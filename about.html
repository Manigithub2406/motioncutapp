ABOUT

Founded in 2018, Delicus has evolved from a humble passion for exceptional culinary experiences to a prominent name in the catering industry. It all began when our visionary founder, MANIKANTA, recognized the transformative power of exquisite food in elevating ordinary events into extraordinary moments.

Driven by a commitment to culinary excellence and a desire to share the joy of delightful dining, Delicus started as a small kitchen venture with a handful of dedicated chefs. From the outset, our mission was clear: to create a catering experience that goes beyond the expected, leaving a lasting impression on every palate we touch.

Over the years, Delicus has grown into a dynamic team of talented chefs, innovative event planners, and service professionals who share a common dedication to perfection. Our journey has been marked by countless celebrations, from intimate family gatherings to high-profile corporate events, each contributing to the rich tapestry of our history.

What sets Delicus apart is not just the sumptuous flavors we serve but the stories we create with every event. We've mastered the delicate art of blending creativity with professionalism, ensuring that each dish is a masterpiece and every occasion is an unforgettable memory.

As we continue to grow, our commitment to tailoring our services to meet the unique needs of our clients remains unwavering. At Delicus, we believe in turning your visions into reality, one delectable bite at a time.

Join us on this journey where history, passion, and flavor converge. Your special moments deserve nothing less than the extraordinary, and at Delicus, we are honored to be a part of your unforgettable celebrations
